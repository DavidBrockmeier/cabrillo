About
Our Property

Our 304 condominium units spread across 65 buildings are just minutes from Orange County's major freeways. The beautifully appointed gardens and lush green landscape are the perfect surroundings for our 2 pool areas, 4 laundry rooms, a workout gym, and large clubhouse. This is Orange County living at its best!

About Cabrillo Park HOA

We were developed and recorded in the Official Records of Orange County California on April 30, 1980, as Track 10580 in the Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions.
