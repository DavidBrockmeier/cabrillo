Upgrade your Storage Bin Locks
×


To reduce the risk of potential vandalism, we are recommending all residents upgrade their storage bin locks. Your storage bins should not include valuables like bicycles, computers, or car rims. Instead, try to limit your stored items to non-essentials like holiday decorations and other easily replaceable items. We also recommend you upgrade your original hinges to the one-way screw options. Vandals will look for the easist opportunity. An ounce of prevention is better than a pound of heartache. Please click here to see sample locks that can be purchased at Home Depot or Lowes. Remember: if you see something...say something.
